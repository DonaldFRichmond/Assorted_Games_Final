This sculpture, The Affe mit Schädel ("ape with skull"), is a famous work by the late-19th-century German sculptor Hugo Rheinhold. The statuette is otherwise known as the "Affe, einen Schädel betrachtend" (monkey viewing or contemplating a skull). It was first exhibited in 1893 at the Grosse Berliner Kunstaustellung (Great Berlin Art Exhibition). This work has long been a favorite of mine. I have had a copy of it since high school. Mine, however, is all brown without the books on which the chimpanzee is sitting upon rendered in color.
